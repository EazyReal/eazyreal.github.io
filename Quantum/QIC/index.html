    bra-ket (Dirac) notation¶

    - relation to state space and dual space - view "bra" as linear map from column vector to scalar - - complex (Hermitian) inner product - http://www.math.umd.edu/~hking/Hermitian.pdf - conjugate symmetric - linear to the 2nd - conjugate linear to the 1st - positive definite

    Note: math => linear to the 1st argument, physics linear to the 2nd
